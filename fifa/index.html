Welcome to Paletools©

Paletools© is a tool to help you with your everyday tasks in EA FC Ultimate Team. It consists of a set of plugins that will assist you in sniping, completing SBCs, opening multiple packs with a single click and keyboard shortcuts for differents tasks among other things.

What can you do with Paletools?
